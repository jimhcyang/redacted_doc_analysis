West Berlin Mayor Opposes Transferring Presidential ElectionWest Berlin's Mayor Willy Brandt informed American officials on 4 June that he was extremely concerned over reports that Bonn was planning to transfer the 1 July West German presidential election from Berlin to Bonn. He feels that since it had been announced that the election, as in 1954, would be held in Berlin, a reversal of plans now would be interpreted as a Western retreat and place a severe strain on West Berlin morale.)
