The USSR is breaking a "gentlemen's agreement" to postpone opening of a diplomatic mission in Nepal. The Indian Government, which regards Nepal as being within its sphere of influence, will not look with favor on this move. Should the United States accept the long-standing Nepali invitation to open an embassy in Katmandu, Communist China--which now has permission to open a consulate in Katmandu--can be expected to press for establishment of an embassy.
