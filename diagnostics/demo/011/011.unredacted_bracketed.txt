ments[[/PRED_REDACTION_1]], who appear determined to maintain an atmosphere of unrest in Paraguay until one of their attacks coincides with [[PRED_REDACTION_2]]suf-
ficient[[/PRED_REDACTION_2]] military and popular discontent to overthrow President Stroessner.
